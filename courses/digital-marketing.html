Digital Marketing

Master the art of promoting businesses online through SEO, content marketing, and paid advertising.

Course Overview

In today's digital world, every business needs online visibility. This course provides comprehensive, hands-on training in digital marketing strategies. You'll learn how to build campaigns that drive traffic, generate leads, and increase sales.

What You Will Learn

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) fundamentals.
  • Social Media Marketing (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn).
  • Running Google Ads and Pay-Per-Click (PPC) campaigns.
  • Content marketing and copywriting basics.
  • Email marketing automation.
  • Analytics and Data Interpretation (Google Analytics).

Skills Gained

SEO Social Media Google Ads Content Marketing Data Analysis

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Digital Marketing Executives, SEO Specialists, Social Media Managers, Paid Search Analysts, or start their own freelance digital marketing agency.
