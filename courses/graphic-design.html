Course Overview

This comprehensive creative program covers the full Adobe design and video editing toolkit. Whether you want to work for a design agency, start your own freelance business, or create content for social media — this course gives you the industry-standard skills employers are looking for.

What You Will Learn

  • Adobe Illustrator – Vector logo and illustration design.
  • Adobe Photoshop – Photo editing, compositing & digital art.
  • Adobe Premiere Pro – Professional video editing & sequencing.
  • Adobe After Effects – Motion graphics & animated titles.
  • Typography, color theory, and design principles.
  • Creating social media posts, banners, and print materials.
  • Building a professional design portfolio.
